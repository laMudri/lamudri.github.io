James Wood

I am a PhD student in Strathclyde's Mathematically Structured Programming (MSP) group, under the supervision of Bob Atkey.

My research project focuses on usage-aware type systems and their formalisation in Agda. In laMudri/quantitative, I work on a simply typed λ-calculus related to Atkey's Quantitative Type Theory (QTT).

I have a list of publications and some blog posts.
